ABOUT US

Fastway Immigration & Visa Services is a Canada-based immigration consultancy offering a wide-range of immigration services to it's clients in Canada and worldwide. Sunil Kumar Malhotra is the founder and director of the firm.

Sunil has 30+ years of extensive past experience working for the largest Public Sector Bank in India, the State Bank of India - rising to the position of Chief Manager. In XXXX, Sunil opted for voluntary retirement from the Senior management capacity to permanently settle in Canada with his family. Thus began a new phase in his life...

Having first-hand experienced the complexities of the Canadian Immigration laws during his process of immigrating to Canada, Sunil decided to utilize his customer service skills and his ability to understand the intricacies of canadian immigration laws to provide Immigration consulting services to people who have recently immigrated to Canada as well as those who intend to visit or settle in Canada in future.

  • He completed Immigration Practitioner Diploma Program from Canadian Society of Immigration Consultants (CSIC).

  • He is also Certified Associate of Indian Institute of bankers with PG diploma in International Business.
