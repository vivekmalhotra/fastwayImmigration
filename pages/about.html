About us

Fastway Immigration & Visa Services is a Canada based immigration firm offering a full range of immigration services to its clients in Canada and worldwide. Sunil Kumar Malhotra, Promoter of the firm, after having rendered outstanding professional service in a Public-Sector Bank in India in senior management capacity opted voluntary retirement and permanently settled in Canada. He completed Immigration Practitioner Diploma Program from Canadian Society of Immigration Consultants (CSIC). He is a Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultant (RCIC) and a member in good standing with Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC), a body regulating Canadian Immigration Consultants. He is also Certified Associate of Indian Institute of bankers with PG diploma in International Business.

We can assist in all matters relating to Immigration, Refugee & Citizenship Law and help individuals, families and businesses settle and establish themselves in Canada. Our services range from obtaining a temporary resident visa for entry into Canada to permanent residence in Canada.

Undoubtedly, immigrating to a new country is a challenging step and life changing experience. In addition, immigration rules are complex and ever-changing. You risk mak ing mistakes, wasting time or increasing the risk of failure of your application by attempting to handle such a complex legal case yourself. To avoid these risks, contact an immigration consultant who is strongly committed to providing you with viable solutions for your immigration problems.

Our certified Immigration Consultant helps making things easier by providing you with all the necessary information/options enabling you to make an informed decision regarding your case for positive outcome. We provide you with an experienced and effective representation to the authorities to present your skills in the best possible way, demonstrating your ability to successfully establish yourself in Canada under immigration laws and regulations.

We are committed and dedicated to providing our clients with competent and professional service with individual approach to each file.

Fastway Immigration & Visa Services provides a range of immigration services to its clients:

  • Hourly consultation (in person or over phone) - If you have any specific question or query, our certified Immigration Consultant can make an in-depth study of your particular situation and advise available options.
  • Submitting Complete Application - Our Certified Immigration Professional can help you filing your complete application forms thereby ensuring that everything is being done correctly and in accordance with requirements of the specific streams.
  • Specific Questions - If you have a few questions and want them to be answered by Certified Immigration Professional OR require us reviewing your application before submission to IRCC, you have option of receiving a consultation before you begin and engage us to review your application.
  • Providing Immigration Plan - Everybody is not aware about the various immigration options available to them as per their profile. We can sift through their profiles and help giving comprehensive immigration plan so that they have a chance to become qualified.
