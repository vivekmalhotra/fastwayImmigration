WHO WE ARE

An Immigration consulting firm based in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada. We provide consultation and representation services to our clients, all around the world, to support them in all their Canadian Immigration, Visa and Citizenship matters. Our consultants are Regulated Canadian Immigration Consultants (RCIC) & member in good standing with Immigration Consultants of Canada Regulatory Council (ICCRC)

OUR COMMITMENT

To provide a quality, timely and cost-effective service to our potential clients. Each client must receive upfront honest assessment about the strengths of their case. Our team will maintain an honest, transparent and open communication with clients and render dedicated service with personal attention to each individual application.
